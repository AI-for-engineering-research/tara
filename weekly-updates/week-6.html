I tried using the teach me skill and had it teach me my research project: the purpose, benefits, and tradeoffs of low-aromatic jet fuel. The conversation is here: teach me skill conversation, and it produced this lesson: The real question is not whether to remove aromatics.

I thought the lesson did a good job clarifying the reality of low-aromatic jet fuel, especially the constraints and downsides. It clearly captured that the correct answer is not simply “lower aromatics means better fuel and lower climate impact.” There are many tradeoffs that need to be assessed.
