Multi-head attention

Core idea: Multiple heads let the model track different kinds of relevance in parallel.

What to remember

  • One head may focus on local syntax while another tracks long-range references.
  • Heads do not need neat human labels to be useful.

Why it matters

A single attention pattern is too limiting for language’s many simultaneous relationships.

Mental model

Same context, several attention mechanisms, several relevance patterns, then combine their outputs.

Checkpoint

  1. Why is one attention head often insufficient?
  2. What does multi-head attention buy conceptually?
