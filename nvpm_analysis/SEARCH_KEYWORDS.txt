YOUR SEARCH STRATEGY (Recommended order)
================================================================================

Day 1-2 (Quick wins, ~2 hours):
  - Run 10 combined searches on Google Scholar, export results
  - Sort by year (2015+), relevance, author
  - Download 20-30 PDFs most likely to have tables

Day 3-5 (Targeted, ~3 hours):
  - Search each major author on ResearchGate
  - Check their "recent publications" + "datasets"
  - Download papers from NTRS directly (often complete data)

Week 2 (Comprehensive, ~4-5 hours):
  - Systematic: for each campaign (AAFEX, ECLIF, ACCESS, SAMPLE)
    search "[Campaign] complete data" OR "[Campaign] final report"
  - Check each author's group website for technical reports
  - Email 2-3 lead authors: "Do you have complete fuel property tables?"

Expected yield:
  Day 1-2: 20-30 new points (if papers have extractable tables)
  Week 2: 80-150 new points (from campaigns you have partial data on)
  Total realistic: +100-150 rows in 1-2 weeks
